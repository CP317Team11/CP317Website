Wilfrid Laurier University

Wilfrid Laurier University (WLU) is a public university in Waterloo, Ontario, Canada, founded in 1911. Known for its strong focus on teaching and community engagement, Laurier offers a variety of programs in business, arts, sciences, and social work. The university is recognized for its supportive environment, experiential learning opportunities, and a vibrant campus life. WLU emphasizes sustainability, leadership, and preparing students for success in their careers.
